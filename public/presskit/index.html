AllCast Mediakit

Feel free to use any of these images.

AllCast lets you send your photos and videos seamlessly to your TV.

Launched on Android in 2014, there are now over 3 million AllCast users sending their cute cat photos to their TV.

Supporting most TV connected devices, AllCast works in any living room:

  • Chromecast
  • Xbox
  • Roku
  • Smart TVs (DLNA)
  • Apple TV
  • Amazon Fire TV

In addition to your camera roll, AllCast can also cast from the most popular cloud storage providers and social networks:

  • Google (Picasa, Google+, Google Drive)
  • Dropbox
  • Media Servers (Plex, Serviio, XBMC)
  • Facebook
  • Instagram
